FAQ

Answers to common questions.

What is LogicDrawer?

LogicDrawer is an interactive, web-based digital logic circuit designer and simulator. Perfect for students, educators, and engineers.

Does it support AI features?

Yes. Generate circuits from sketches or natural language, then refine with auto-wiring and validation.

What components are included?

Logic gates, adders, flip-flops, multiplexers, displays, and more—with analysis tools like truth tables, K‑maps, and signal flow visualization.

Can I import/export designs?

Export to Verilog, JSON, PNG, PDF. Import from supported formats as you iterate.
